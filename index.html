A Workshop on Dhvanyaloka

Last Date to apply: January 28, 2020
Free accommodation,TA and No Registration Fee Registration

Objective

The Workshop aims to explore the poetic world through the method of Text-Reading of Dhvanyaloka of Anandavardhan by deliberating on the commentary of Abhinavagupta.

"The study of the Scriptures is useless so long as the highest Truth is unknown, and it is equally useless when the highest Truth has already been known"
-- The Upanishads
